# Dashboard Overview

The Friction dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your brand's performance across AI platforms, divided into two main sections: **Your Custom Analyses** (user-created content) and **Brand Health** (Friction-generated insights).


## Two-Section Layout

### Section 1: Your Custom Analyses

**Manually-Defined** content from prompts you create in the Define and Commerce pages.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Top Prompts & Search Queries" icon="list">
    Shows your highest-performing user-created prompts from the Define page, ranked by visibility score.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Commerce Prompts" icon="cart-shopping">
    Displays your commerce-specific prompts with purchase intent metrics and e-commerce platform visibility.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top Sources" icon="link">
    Lists the most frequently cited domains across your prompts (e.g., your website, competitor sites, industry publications).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Experiments" icon="flask">
    Preview of your active and recent A/B experiments testing different prompt strategies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Section 2: Brand Health

**Friction-Generated** metrics based on system prompts automatically created during onboarding.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="4 Quick Metrics" icon="gauge">
    Visibility, Authority, Sentiment, and Purchase Intent scores with trend indicators
  </Card>
  <Card title="Performance Trends" icon="chart-line">
    Historical chart showing how all 4 metrics have changed over the selected time period
  </Card>
  <Card title="LLM Performance" icon="brain">
    Comparison of your brand's performance across different AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Industry Ranking" icon="trophy">
    Leaderboard showing your brand's position vs. competitors in your industry
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strategic Recommendations" icon="lightbulb">
    AI-generated actionable insights to improve your brand's AI presence
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Time Period Filters

Both sections have independent time period filters:

- 7 Days
- 14 Days
- 30 Days (default)

Changing the period updates all metrics, charts, and trends within that section.

## Understanding the Data Split

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Custom Analyses">
    **Source**: Your manually created prompts

    **Purpose**: Track specific queries you care about

    **Control**: You decide what to track

    **Examples**:
    - "What is [Your Brand]?"
    - "Best [product] for [use case]"
    - "[Product name] reviews"

    **Use Cases**:
    - Monitor specific product queries
    - Track brand awareness questions
    - Measure commerce intent
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Brand Health">
    **Source**: System-generated prompts (~25 prompts created during onboarding)

    **Purpose**: Measure overall brand health across 4 key metrics

    **Control**: Automatically managed by Friction

    **Metrics**:
    1. **Visibility** - How often your brand appears
    2. **Authority** - Trust and credibility signals
    3. **Sentiment** - Positive/neutral/negative perception
    4. **Purchase Intent** - Recommendation strength

    **Use Cases**:
    - Executive reporting
    - Competitive benchmarking
    - Trend analysis
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Performance Trends Chart

Interactive line chart with:
- All 4 metrics plotted over time
- Click-to-filter: Click a metric name in the legend to show/hide it
- Hover tooltips showing exact values
- Period selection (7/14/30 days)


### LLM Performance Chart

Comparison across AI models:
- GPT-4 / GPT-4o Mini
- Claude Haiku / Claude Sonnet
- Gemini Flash / Gemini Pro
- Perplexity
- Google AI Search

Shows which models rate your brand highest/lowest.

### Industry Ranking

Compact leaderboard showing:
- Your brand's position (highlighted)
- Top 5 brands in your industry
- Scores for each brand
- Your market position


### Strategic Recommendations

AI-generated insights categorized by:
- **Quick Wins**: Easy improvements with high impact
- **Strategic Priorities**: Longer-term initiatives
- **Competitive Alerts**: Where competitors are outperforming you
- **Maintenance Reminders**: Regular tasks to maintain performance

## Empty States

### First-Time User Experience

After completing onboarding, you'll see:

1. **Custom Analyses Section**:
   - Empty state prompts to "Add Prompts" or "Get Prompt Recommendations"
   - Top Sources and Experiments cards are collapsed (no data yet)

2. **Brand Health Section**:
   - Loading skeleton while initial analysis runs
   - Full metrics appear within a few minutes

<Info>
  **Pro Tip**: Start by adding 5-10 custom prompts in the Define page to populate your Custom Analyses section, then let the Brand Health metrics run automatically.
</Info>

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can:

- **Add Brand Prompts**: Click any "Add Prompts" button → redirects to `/define/add`
- **Add Commerce Prompts**: Click "Get Recommendations" → redirects to `/commerce/add`
- **View Full Analyze Page**: Click any metric card to see detailed breakdown
- **Manage Experiments**: Click experiment cards to view/edit

## Refresh Behavior

The dashboard refreshes automatically when:
- You switch brands via the sidebar
- Analysis completes in the background
- You return from adding new prompts

<Note>
  Analysis typically takes 2-5 minutes to complete. The dashboard shows loading skeletons while fetching data, then smoothly transitions to showing results.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Monitor Weekly">
    Check your dashboard weekly to catch trends early
  </Step>
  <Step title="Focus on Trends">
    The direction of change matters more than absolute scores
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare Metrics">
    Low visibility but high sentiment? You need more content. High visibility but low purchase intent? Improve conversion messaging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on Recommendations">
    The Strategic Recommendations panel provides specific, actionable steps
  </Step>
</Steps>
