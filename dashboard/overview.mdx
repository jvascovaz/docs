Welcome to your Friction dashboard - the central hub for monitoring and optimizing your brand's AI performance across all major language models.

## What is Friction?

Friction is an AI-powered platform that helps you understand and improve how your brand appears in AI-generated responses. As AI becomes the primary interface for information discovery, Friction ensures your brand maintains strong visibility and positive sentiment.

## Dashboard Layout

The Friction dashboard is organized into several key sections:

Located on the left side of your screen, the sidebar provides quick access to:

- **Overview**: Your main dashboard with key metrics

## Key Metrics

### AI Performance Score
A composite score (0-100) that measures your overall brand performance in AI responses.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is it calculated?">
    The AI Performance Score combines multiple factors:
    - **Visibility (40%)**: How often your brand appears in relevant queries
    - **Sentiment (25%)**: The tone and context of brand mentions
    - **Authority (20%)**: Recognition as a category leader
    - **Intent (15%)**: Likelihood of recommendation or purchase suggestion
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's a good score?">
    - **80-100**: Excellent - Strong AI presence and positive sentiment
    - **60-79**: Good - Above average visibility with room for improvement
    - **40-59**: Average - Moderate presence, optimization needed
    - **Below 40**: Needs Attention - Significant optimization required
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Brand Visibility
Measures how frequently your brand appears in AI-generated responses for relevant queries.

### Sentiment Analysis
Tracks the emotional tone and context surrounding your brand mentions:
- **Positive**: Recommendations, praise, preferred choices
- **Neutral**: Factual mentions, listings, comparisons
- **Negative**: Criticisms, warnings, or unfavorable comparisons

### Purchase Intent
Analyzes whether AI models recommend your brand for purchase decisions.

## Real-time Monitoring

The dashboard updates every 15 minutes with fresh data from ongoing AI interactions.

<Note>
  Real-time monitoring covers the top 1,000 queries relevant to your industry and brand keywords.
</Note>

### Alert System

Configure alerts for:
- Sudden drops in visibility (>20% decrease)
- Negative sentiment spikes
- Competitor surges
- New AI model deployments affecting your brand

## Competitive Analysis

Track up to 10 competitor brands simultaneously to:
- Benchmark your AI performance
- Identify competitive advantages
- Discover content gaps
- Monitor industry trends

<Info>
  Competitive data is normalized to ensure fair comparisons across brands of different sizes.
</Info>

## Optimization

Based on your dashboard insights, Friction provides:

1. **Content Recommendations**: Specific content types that improve AI visibility
2. **Keyword Opportunities**: Terms that boost brand appearance
3. **Sentiment Improvements**: Strategies to enhance positive mentions
4. **Technical Optimizations**: Website and data structure improvements
