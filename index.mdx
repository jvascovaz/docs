## What Can You Do With Friction?

### 1. Define Your Brand Visibility

Create custom prompts to track how AI models respond to queries about your brand, products, and industry.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Brand Prompts">
    Define queries that matter to your business - from "What is [your brand]?" to "Best [product category] for [use case]"
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI-Powered Suggestions">
    Get intelligent prompt recommendations based on your industry and competitors
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-Time Analysis">
    See how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other models respond to your prompts
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Analyze Your Performance

Track four critical brand health metrics across all AI platforms:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Visibility">
    Measures how frequently your brand appears in AI-generated responses. Tracks your presence across different AI models and platforms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Authority">
    Measures recognition as a category leader or trusted source. Analyzes source quality, citation context, and brand expertise signals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sentiment">
    Tracks the tone and context of brand mentions - positive, neutral, or negative. Understand how AI models perceive your brand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Purchase Intent">
    Analyzes whether AI models recommend your brand for purchase decisions. Measures recommendation strength and use case matching.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### 3. Compare Against Competitors

See how your brand stacks up:

- Industry ranking and positioning
- Competitive gap analysis
- Source citation comparison
- Trend analysis over time

### 4. Optimize for Commerce

Track commerce-specific metrics:

- Product recommendations
- Purchase intent signals
- E-commerce platform mentions
- Shopping-related queries

### 5. Run A/B Experiments

Test different content strategies:

- Compare prompt variations
- Measure impact of content updates
- Track experiment results over time
- Make data-driven optimization decisions
